Compact Modeling of GaN HEMTs with Gate and Source Field Plates

The outcome of one of Sahra Ebrahimmagham and her team members research has been the development of a comprehensive physics-based compact model for GaN HEMTs incorporating both Gate Field Plates (GFP) and Source Field Plates (SFP). The model captures the coupled electrostatic interactions between multiple field-plate structures and accurately predicts their influence on device performance.

The model includes the effects of

Electric-field redistribution
Surface potential modulation
Charge transport
Dynamic trapping and detrapping
Capacitance variation
Breakdown voltage enhancement
Self-heating
Temperature dependence
Switching losses
High-frequency operation

By integrating both GFP and SFP effects into a unified compact modeling framework, this work provides an accurate and scalable solution for the simulation of advanced GaN power devices and RF transistors.
