Vision for Future Semiconductor Technologies

As wide-bandgap semiconductor technologies continue to evolve, compact models must evolve alongside increasingly sophisticated device architectures.

My research aims to establish scalable, predictive, and physically grounded compact modeling techniques that support the design of future high-performance electronic systems for electric vehicles, renewable energy, aerospace, telecommunications, and advanced integrated circuits.

Through continued collaboration between academia and industry, my goal is to contribute to the next generation of compact models that enable faster technology development, more reliable circuit simulation, and broader adoption of advanced GaN transistor technologies.
