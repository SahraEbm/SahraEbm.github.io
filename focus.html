Physics-Based Compact Modeling of GaN HEMTs

My research combines semiconductor device physics with compact model development to accurately describe GaN HEMT behavior under DC, transient, RF, and power-switching operating conditions.

Current research topics include

Surface-potential-based compact modeling
Charge-based transistor modeling
Electrostatic modeling
Self-heating effects
Charge trapping and current collapse
Dynamic ON-resistance
Temperature-dependent behavior
Breakdown mechanisms
High-frequency parasitic effects
