About Me

Sahra Ebrahimmagham is a semiconductor device engineer and researcher specializing in compact modeling, GaN HEMT devices, and next-generation transistor architectures.

Her work focuses on bridging device physics with circuit-level simulation through physically informed Verilog-A models, enabling accurate prediction of device behavior in RF and power electronics applications.

Research Interests

  • • Physics-based Compact Modeling (Verilog-A, SPICE)
  • • GaN High Electron Mobility Transistors (HEMTs)
  • • Wide Bandgap Semiconductors
  • • Gate-All-Around & 3D Architectures
