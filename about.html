Research Focus

My research centers on the physics-based compact modeling of GaN HEMTs with gate field-plate structures. I extended the renowned EPFL GaN HEMT model by incorporating field-plate electrostatics and access-region resistances, creating a robust charge-based framework for power electronics.

This work has been submitted to IEEE Transactions on Electron Devices and is currently under review. The model is validated against both TCAD simulations and experimental data.

In parallel, I am developing a 2-D analytical model of AlGaN/GaN HEMTs with field-plate structures to provide fundamental insights into device electrostatics and charge transport mechanisms.
