Universal Compact Modeling for Arbitrary Field-Plate Architectures

Sahra Ebrahimmagham and her research team developed a generalized compact modeling framework capable of describing GaN HEMTs with arbitrary field-plate configurations.

Such a universal compact model would provide a unified simulation framework...

adaptable to emerging GaN transistor designs without requiring a new compact model for each device architecture. This approach aims to accelerate semiconductor innovation while enabling accurate circuit simulation across a broad range of next-generation wide-bandgap devices.

The objective is to accurately represent

  • • Single field-plate structures
  • • Dual field-plate architectures
  • • Multiple field-plate devices
  • • Gate-connected field plates
  • • Source-connected field plates
  • • Drain-connected field plates
  • • Hybrid field-plate configurations
  • • Future field-plate geometries
